How to Play

  1. Click on any card to flip it and reveal its symbol
  2. Click on another card to find its matching pair
  3. If the two cards match, they will remain face up
  4. If they don't match, they will flip back over
  5. Find all matching pairs with the fewest moves and fastest time!
